Frequently Asked Questions

What types of yoga do you offer?

We offer a variety of yoga styles including Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, and Restorative yoga. Each class is designed to cater to different needs and experience levels.

Do I need to bring my own yoga mat?

While we encourage bringing your own mat for hygiene reasons, we do provide mats for those who need them. We sanitize all studio equipment regularly.

Are your classes suitable for beginners?

Yes, we have classes suitable for all levels, including complete beginners. Our instructors are trained to provide modifications and alternatives for different skill levels.

How often should I practice yoga?

The frequency of practice depends on your personal goals and schedule. For beginners, we recommend starting with 2-3 classes per week. As you progress, you may find benefit in practicing more frequently.

Do you offer private yoga sessions?

Yes, we offer private one-on-one and small group sessions. These can be tailored to your specific needs and goals. Contact us for more information and pricing.
